GPX Leuven overview map

This is an overview of cycling routes that start at the Dataroots offices in the vincinity of the railstation of Leuven. Most roads are well paved with at most one or few segments of gravel. Hence for this roads a race bike is recommended.

  • Click on the title above to get an overview map.
  • Click on the title of the routes to get a detail for every route on a map and a height profile. For each route, the number of climbs are counted and indicated. Climbs are defined as those ascents with a climb score above 1500. The climb score is defined as the product of the lenght with the average grade of the climb.
  • Click on the download button to download the corresponding gpx file.

The cycling routes are made with love by Lode Nachtergaele. They serve the cycling enthousiast at Dataroots that enjoy a weekly afterwork ride. If you want to join those rides, join our Strava club.

Climbs are automatically detected using Scipy's find_peaks function. In case you need a height profile for your own route, upload your GPX file to this Hugging Face application. It will generate a map and height profiles similare to those here.
